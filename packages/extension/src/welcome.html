Welcome to iamtoolazy

Three things worth 60 seconds — then it stays out of your way.

1 What it does

Every message to Claude / ChatGPT / Gemini costs tokens. Press Alt+L in the chat box and iamtoolazy trims the filler — without touching code, URLs, numbers, or negations — and shows you honest numbers about it. Short prompts? It tells you they're already lean and does nothing.

2 Pick a mode per site

preview shows a diff first (recommended) · auto applies instantly with Undo · off disables the shortcut there.

3 Your data

Everything lives in this browser's local storage: your per-site modes and a small ledger of savings (all numbers labeled estimates). Zero telemetry, no accounts, and the extension makes no network calls at all — nothing ever leaves your machine.
Source, docs, and the honest-numbers policy: github.com/MRLNT/iamtoolazy
